Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Metro Linkz and our ecosystem

What is Metro Linkz Inc.?

Metro Linkz Inc. is a next-generation technology company and parent ecosystem powering multiple innovative products, platforms, and services designed to push the boundaries of digital finance, cybersecurity, and technological empowerment. We are headquartered in Lagos with a global vision.

What is Metro Coin (MTC)?

Metro Coin (MTC) is the exclusive digital currency of the Metro Linkz ecosystem. It has a fixed supply of 21,600,000 coins and serves as the primary medium of exchange across all Metro Linkz platforms. MTC is designed to appreciate in value as adoption increases.

How can I acquire MTC?

You can acquire MTC through several methods: participating in the Metro Miner program, completing tasks on Leancnstask, purchasing through authorized exchanges (coming soon), or through our Lean Business agent network.

What makes Metro Linkz different from other tech companies?

Metro Linkz stands out through our comprehensive ecosystem approach, combining financial technology with advanced cybersecurity. We focus on both empowering users financially and securing their digital assets, with a specific mission to drive technological adoption across Africa.

How does Metro Wallet ensure security?

Metro Wallet employs multiple security layers including military-grade encryption, multi-signature technology, biometric authentication, and AI-driven fraud detection. The wallet will evolve through several versions, with V4.5.0 offering next-gen decentralized security protocols.

How can I become part of the Lean Business program?

To join our Lean Business agent network, you can apply through our official website. The program provides tools, training, and rewards for individuals and organizations interested in promoting Metro Linkz products and expanding our ecosystem.
